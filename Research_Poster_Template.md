## Formatting Guidelines

### Typography
- **Title:** Large, bold, easily readable from 5+ feet
- **Section Headers:** Bold, clearly distinguished from body text
- **Body Text:** Readable from 3–4 feet away

### Figures & Graphs
- All figures should be high-resolution and vectorized when possible
- Include captions and labels on all axes
- MATLAB plots: export using `print –depsc2 filename.eps`

### Design
- Cohesive color scheme (university branding preferred)
- Consistent formatting throughout all sections
- White or light background for readability
- Logical flow: Problem → Methods/Data → Results → Conclusion

### Content Sections Checklist
- [ ] Problem Statement — clearly defines the research problem
- [ ] Dataset — describes the data sources and characteristics
- [ ] Methods/Story — explains the approach and methodology
- [ ] Results — presents findings with appropriate figures
- [ ] Conclusion — summarizes key takeaways and learned outcomes
- [ ] References — properly cited sources
